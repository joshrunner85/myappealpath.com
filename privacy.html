What We Collect

AppealPath does not require you to create an account and does not collect your name, email address, or personal health information.

What Stays on Your Device

  • Conversation history with Patient Advocate
  • Purchase status and question counter
  • App preferences and settings

This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to AppealPath servers.

Patient Advocate Messages

When you send a message to Patient Advocate, that message and relevant conversation context are transmitted to OpenAI's API to generate a response. This transmission is subject to OpenAI's privacy policy, available at openai.com/privacy. Do not include sensitive personal health information such as Social Security numbers, Medicare numbers, or detailed medical records in your Patient Advocate messages.

In-App Purchases

Purchase transactions are processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). AppealPath does not receive or store your payment information.

Analytics

AppealPath may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand how features are used in aggregate (no personally identifiable data). No advertising networks are used. No data is sold.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: support@myappealpath.com

AppealPath is an educational tool. It is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice.
