Why appeal at all

Denials get overturned more than people think

Inpatient rehab (IRF) denials are especially winnable when appealed correctly — which is why AppealPath focuses heavily on IRF, even though the same process applies broadly.

80%+

At some Medicare Advantage plans, more than 80% of appealed IRF (inpatient rehab) denials were overturned — averaging 43% across the three largest plans overall. Denied care is often medically necessary care.

Source: HHS Office of Inspector General, Report No. OEI-09-24-00330 (June 2026)
